Artist Statement

When it comes to my art, it really just pops up in my head whenever I come up with an idea and so I get motivated to create something that I have thought of. At times I get inspiration from other art which helps me do my best to make a unique version of my own within that style of art. I mainly like vector drawing on adobe illustrator because I’ve been using that application since I was a sophomore in high school. I was taught the basic components of it while at school & so I worked my way up to create more art then eventually improving on it. Though I do like making art from illustrator, I do find more interest in the art of film. I feel as if I can tell a story of my own because I am a very visual person. I’m still in the process of perfecting my craft in that category but I have some videos up on my YouTube Channel called MarcTheBro. Obviously it’s not the best videos out there but I try to put a lot of effort into those videos instead of having something that just looks decent & then posting it for attention. My whole purpose for creating film is to motivate other young filmmakers like me to perfect their craft & feel inspired so that they can achieve this dream of theirs in the art of film. I’d like to be a guide for people who are lost & are trying their best to try and find ways they can become better artists. I would also like to one reach my full potential in this category because I set goals for myself & everyone who supports my journey are the reason why I am getting this far, a reason why I have hope and I am not giving up.

–Marc Mallari
